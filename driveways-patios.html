FAQ — Driveways & Patios

How long do paver driveways last in Miami?

Properly installed paver driveways last 25–50 years in Florida's climate. They resist cracking because individual pavers can shift independently, unlike solid concrete. Resealing every 3–5 years extends their life indefinitely.

What's the best material for a Miami pool deck?

Travertine is the gold standard — it stays significantly cooler than concrete, is slip-resistant, and ages beautifully. Pavers are also excellent. We advise based on your budget and aesthetic preferences.

How long does a driveway installation take?

Standard residential driveways take 2–5 days. Larger projects with pool decks and patios may take 1–2 weeks. We minimize disruption and protect your landscaping throughout.

Do you offer financing for exterior projects?

Yes — we offer flexible financing for qualified customers. Ask about our low monthly payment options when you request your estimate.
