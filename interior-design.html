Espacios Diseñados para Inspirar, Construidos para Durar

Interior design isn't decoration — it's the art of making a space feel exactly the way you want to feel when you walk through the door. At Stratton Trademark Company, our designers combine architectural understanding with an instinct for beauty to create Miami interiors that are uniquely, unmistakably yours. From concept sketches to final furniture placement, we handle everything.

El diseño de interiores no es decoración — es el arte de hacer que un espacio se sienta exactamente como usted quiere. Nuestros diseñadores combinan comprensión arquitectónica con instinto para la belleza.

  • Space Planning & LayoutWe rethink how rooms flow together — opening walls, creating sight lines, and maximizing every square foot.
  • Material & Finish SelectionFlooring, paint colors, tile, countertops, hardware — curated palettes that create cohesion throughout your home.
  • Custom MillworkBuilt-in shelving, entertainment walls, coffered ceilings, wainscoting — architectural details that add irreplaceable value.
  • Lighting DesignLayered lighting schemes with ambient, task, and accent lighting controlled by smart dimmers and switches.
  • Color ConsultingPaint color selection that considers your lighting, furniture, and the Miami sun — colors that photograph and feel beautiful.
  • Project ManagementOne point of contact who coordinates every trade, every delivery, every installation. You stay stress-free.

Our Design Process

Discovery Meeting

We learn your style, lifestyle, budget, and must-haves. We look at inspiration photos together and develop a shared creative direction.

Concept Presentation

We present mood boards, material palettes, 3D renderings, and a complete scope of work with itemized pricing. No surprises.

Material Procurement

We source and order all materials, coordinating delivery timing so your project flows without delays or holds.

Coordinated Build

Our in-house carpenters, painters, tile setters, and electricians work on a choreographed schedule. Zero overlap, zero downtime.

Styling & Reveal

Final accessories, art hanging, and furniture arrangement. You walk through your home seeing it complete for the first time.

FAQ — Interior Design

Do you have an in-house designer?

Yes — our design team works exclusively with Stratton Trademark Company projects. This means your designer is fully coordinated with your construction team from day one.

Can you work with my existing furniture?

Absolutely. We design around what you have and help you identify which pieces to keep, update, or replace to achieve the look you want within your budget.

How much does interior design cost?

Design fees vary by project scope. For full renovation projects, design is often included in the construction contract. We offer standalone design consultations starting at a flat fee — contact us for current pricing.

Do you only serve Miami?

We serve all of Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties — from Florida City to West Palm Beach.
