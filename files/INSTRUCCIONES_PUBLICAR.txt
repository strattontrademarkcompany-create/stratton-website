PASO 1 — PUBLICAR EN NETLIFY
=================================================================
1. Ve a https://netlify.com
2. Sign up con tu email (usa info@strattonremodeling.com)
3. En el dashboard clic en "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
4. SELECCIONA TODOS LOS ARCHIVOS de esta carpeta y arrástralos
   (index.html + netlify.toml + robots.txt + sitemap.xml)
5. Netlify te da un link temporal como: abc123.netlify.app ✅

=================================================================
PASO 2 — CONECTAR DOMINIO GODADDY
=================================================================
EN NETLIFY:
1. Ve a Site Settings → Domain Management → Add custom domain
2. Escribe: strattonremodeling.com → Verify → Add domain
3. Netlify te mostrará sus nameservers. Anótalos:
   - dns1.p05.nsone.net
   - dns2.p05.nsone.net
   - dns3.p05.nsone.net
   - dns4.p05.nsone.net
   (pueden variar — usa los que Netlify te indique)

EN GODADDY:
1. Ve a godaddy.com → My Products → strattonremodeling.com → DNS
2. Scroll hasta "Nameservers" → clic "Change"
3. Selecciona "Enter my own nameservers"
4. Pega los nameservers de Netlify
5. Guarda → espera 1-24 horas

=================================================================
PASO 3 — HTTPS/SSL (AUTOMÁTICO EN NETLIFY)
=================================================================
1. En Netlify → Site Settings → Domain Management
2. Scroll hasta "HTTPS" → clic "Verify DNS configuration"
3. Luego "Provision certificate" → LISTO ✅
   (Netlify provee SSL gratis con Let's Encrypt)

=================================================================
PASO 4 — GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE
=================================================================
1. Ve a https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Clic "Add property" → escribe: https://www.strattonremodeling.com
3. Elige verificación por "HTML tag"
4. Copia el meta tag que te dan (algo así):
   <meta name="google-site-verification" content="XXXXXXX">
5. Díselo a Claude para agregarlo al index.html
6. Vuelve a subir el archivo a Netlify
7. En Search Console clic "Verify" ✅
8. Envía el sitemap: https://www.strattonremodeling.com/sitemap.xml

=================================================================
PASO 5 — GOOGLE ANALYTICS
=================================================================
1. Ve a https://analytics.google.com
2. Crea cuenta → nombre: "Stratton Trademark Company"
3. Crea propiedad → nombre: strattonremodeling.com
4. Copia tu Measurement ID (formato: G-XXXXXXXXXX)
5. Díselo a Claude para agregarlo al index.html
