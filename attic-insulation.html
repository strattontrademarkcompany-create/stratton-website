The Science Behind Attic Insulation in South Florida

La Ciencia del Aislamiento de Áticos en el Sur de Florida

🌡️

Blown-In Fiberglass

The most common and cost-effective solution for Florida attics. We install to R-38 or higher as required by Florida Building Code, covering every corner and edge without gaps.

🔬

Spray Foam (Open & Closed Cell)

The premium solution that insulates AND air-seals in one application. Closed-cell spray foam also adds structural rigidity and provides a moisture barrier critical in humid Miami.

✨

Radiant Barrier Foil

Installed under the roof decking, radiant barriers reflect up to 97% of solar radiation before it converts to heat. Combined with insulation, this is the ultimate South Florida solution.

🌀

Air Sealing Package

Before any insulation goes in, we seal attic floor penetrations with foam — light fixtures, plumbing, electrical boxes — stopping the #1 cause of conditioned air loss in Florida homes.

🗑️

Old Insulation Removal

Rodent-infested, water-damaged, or simply depleted old insulation removed safely with HEPA vacuum equipment. We sanitize before installing fresh material.

⚡

FPL Rebate Assistance

Florida Power & Light offers rebates for qualifying insulation upgrades. We help you identify and apply for available rebates to maximize your return on investment.

FAQ — Attic Insulation

What R-value do I need for my Miami home?

Florida Building Code requires R-38 minimum for attics in Miami-Dade. Energy Star recommends R-49 to R-60 for maximum savings in our climate. We'll show you the cost difference and payback period for each level.

How do I know if my insulation needs replacing?

Signs include: high FPL bills, rooms that are difficult to cool, visible old or thin insulation in the attic, an AC system that runs constantly. Our free assessment will give you a definitive answer.

How much can I save on my energy bill?

Miami homeowners typically see a 20–40% reduction in cooling costs after a proper attic insulation upgrade. With FPL's average rates, this can mean $100–$200+ in monthly savings for larger homes.

How long does attic insulation installation take?

Most residential attic insulation projects complete in a single day. Larger homes or projects involving old insulation removal may take 2 days. We cause minimal disruption to your household.

Is attic insulation worth it in Miami?

Absolutely — it's one of the highest-ROI home improvements you can make in South Florida. With our heat and AC costs, properly insulating your attic typically pays for itself within 1–3 years through energy savings alone.
