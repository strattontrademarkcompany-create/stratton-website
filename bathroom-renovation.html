FAQ — Bathroom Renovations

How long does a bathroom renovation take?

A standard full bathroom renovation takes 2–3 weeks. Master baths with custom tile work can take 4–5 weeks. We provide a guaranteed completion date in writing.

How much does a bathroom renovation cost in Miami?

Guest bath refreshes start around $6,000–$12,000. Full master bath renovations typically range from $18,000–$45,000+. We offer financing with monthly payments.

Do you handle both plumbing and tile?

Yes — we are a full-service contractor. Our in-house plumbers, tile setters, and electricians work as one coordinated team, eliminating coordination headaches.

Can you match existing tile if I only want a partial renovation?

In most cases yes. We source tile from over 40 suppliers and can often find exact or near-exact matches. We'll assess and advise you honestly.
