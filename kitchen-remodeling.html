Frequently Asked Questions

Preguntas Frecuentes

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

Most kitchen remodels in Miami take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. A full gut renovation with custom cabinets may take 10–12 weeks. We provide a precise timeline in your contract.

Do I need permits for a kitchen remodel?

Any work involving structural changes, electrical, or plumbing requires permits in Miami-Dade. We handle all permitting — you never have to visit a government office.

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Miami?

Kitchen remodels in Miami typically range from $15,000 (cosmetic refresh) to $80,000+ (full custom build). We offer financing options to fit most budgets. Contact us for a free estimate.

Can I stay in my home during the remodel?

Yes. We set up temporary kitchen access when possible and seal work areas with plastic barriers to contain dust. Most clients stay home throughout the project.
