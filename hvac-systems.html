FAQ — HVAC Systems

How often should I replace my AC system in Miami?

Most AC systems in Miami last 10–15 years due to the heavy usage demands of South Florida's climate. If your system is over 10 years old and requires frequent repairs, replacement is almost always more cost-effective.

What SEER rating should I choose for Miami?

In Miami's climate, we recommend at least 16 SEER2. Higher efficiency systems (18–20 SEER2) cost more upfront but can reduce your FPL bill by hundreds of dollars per year. We'll show you the payback calculation.

Do I need a permit to replace my AC in Miami?

Yes — any AC replacement in Miami-Dade or Broward requires a permit and inspection. We handle all permitting and coordinate the final inspection so you're fully compliant.

How much does a new central AC system cost in Miami?

A new central AC system in Miami typically costs $4,000–$10,000+ depending on the size of your home and the efficiency level. We offer financing options with low monthly payments.
